Chinese Scientists Set New World Record in Solar Cell Efficiency

中国科学家刷新太阳能电池效率世界纪录

📅 July 23, 2026 📖 Nature Journal ⏱️ 约8分钟阅读 今日最新

📌 文章摘要

2026年7月13日，中国科学院化学研究所团队在《自然》杂志发表突破性研究成果：新型钙钛矿-有机叠层太阳能电池认证效率达28.04%，刷新世界纪录。该技术通过创新的"全阶段调控"策略解决了材料稳定性难题，为未来轻薄柔性光伏应用开辟新道路，展现了中国在清洁能源领域的领先地位。

A Revolutionary Breakthrough

In a groundbreaking development published in the prestigious journal Nature on July 13, 2026, Chinese scientists from the Institute of Chemistry, Chinese Academy of Sciences have achieved a remarkable milestone in renewable energy research. Their newly developed perovskite-organic tandem solar cell has reached a certified steady-state power conversion efficiency of 28.04%, setting a new world record for this class of solar cells.

The research team, led by Academician Li Yongfang and Researcher Meng Lei, has pioneered what they call an "all-stage regulation" strategy. This innovative approach addresses one of the most challenging problems in solar cell technology: the instability of light-sensitive materials under continuous illumination. By introducing a unique light-transformable additive molecule named TDB, the team has successfully overcome the phase separation issue that has long hindered the commercial application of such devices.

The Science Behind the Innovation

Traditional single-junction solar cells, commonly seen in household or power station crystalline silicon panels, rely on a single light-absorbing material. However, this limitation prevents them from efficiently utilizing photons with different energy levels simultaneously. The perovskite-organic tandem solar cell developed by the Chinese team employs a sophisticated sandwich-like structure where two photovoltaic materials are stacked together.

"The upper perovskite layer absorbs visible light, while the lower organic layer captures near-infrared light. This design allows the device to 'squeeze every drop' from the solar spectrum, theoretically achieving far higher power generation efficiency than single-material batteries."

However, creating such tandem devices presented significant challenges. The upper layer requires both iodine and bromine elements to absorb sufficient sunlight. Unfortunately, during manufacturing or under continuous light exposure, these ions tend to separate and form clusters—a phenomenon known as phase separation. Once this occurs, the battery voltage continuously drops, and performance steadily deteriorates, becoming a fatal weakness preventing practical application.

From "Fearing Light" to "Harnessing Light"

To resolve this microscopic conflict, the research team introduced the TDB additive molecule into the material. During the initial formation of the battery film, TDB acts as a patient coordinator, slowing down the rapid aggregation of bromine ions and ensuring that iodine and bromine remain uniformly mixed from the beginning.

When the battery begins operating under light, TDB is photo-activated and transforms into a new molecular structure called TAB. This new molecule can anchor at the material's grain boundaries, inhibiting halide ion migration and phase separation. As Meng Lei explains, this represents a complete transformation from "fearing light" to "harnessing light."

Impressive Performance Results

The results have been extraordinary. The wide-bandgap perovskite solar cell created through this strategy achieved the highest open-circuit voltage record for its type. When precisely stacked with the underlying organic solar cell, the entire tandem device reached a laboratory maximum power conversion efficiency of 28.80%, with certified steady-state efficiency locked at 28.04%. Furthermore, after 625 hours of continuous illumination, the tandem solar cell still maintained 90% of its initial efficiency, demonstrating excellent stability.

Li Yongfang emphasized that this perovskite-organic tandem solar cell combines the advantages of being lightweight and flexible. It holds promise not only for applications in architecture, transportation, and wearable devices but also, due to its outstanding flexibility-to-weight ratio, has potential to be deployed on satellites and space stations, providing lighter and more efficient space-based energy for humanity.

Global Context and Future Implications

This achievement underscores China's leadership in clean energy technology innovation. According to the Global Electricity Development Index Report released at the United Nations on July 10, 2026, China ranks at the forefront in technological innovation among 100 evaluated countries. The report assesses nations across four dimensions: supply security, consumption services, green and low-carbon development, and technological innovation.

As the world continues transitioning toward sustainable energy sources, breakthroughs like this represent critical steps toward addressing climate change and reducing dependence on fossil fuels. The manufacturing process for these next-generation solar cells is particularly noteworthy—they can be produced like newspapers, using solution processing and roll-to-roll printing technologies, enabling flexible, large-scale production with lightweight, bendable final products.

The research received support from multiple institutions and represents years of dedicated scientific investigation. With the Chinese government's Renewable Energy Development "15th Five-Year Plan" released on July 23, 2026, explicitly calling for accelerated research on perovskite tandem cell industrialization, the pathway from laboratory to commercial application appears increasingly clear.

• 主句：Their newly developed perovskite-organic tandem solar cell has reached...efficiency of 28.04%

• 翻译：他们新开发的钙钛矿-有机叠层太阳能电池达到了28.04%的认证稳态光电转换效率，刷新了该类太阳能电池的世界纪录。

2. "By introducing a unique light-transformable additive molecule named TDB, the team has successfully overcome the phase separation issue that has long hindered the commercial application of such devices."

• 时间状语从句：When the battery begins operating under light

• 现在分词短语：inhibiting...作结果状语

• 翻译：当电池在光照下开始工作时，TDB被光激活并转化为一种名为TAB的新分子结构，它可以锚定在材料晶界上，抑制卤素离子迁移和相分离。
